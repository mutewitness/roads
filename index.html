Road system generation with conflicting evaluators

This application will attempt to find the most optimal way to connect roads between the cities on the map as to minimize the cost function.

Use the sliders below to adjust how the cost is computed by changing the respective weights given to certain characteristics of the solution.

You can drag cities around.
